Quick and Easy Spaghetti Bolognese

This fast-track recipe for Quick & Easy Spaghetti Bolognese makes a meat sauce thatâ€™s bursting with Italian flavor and cooks in just 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil (2 tbsp)
  • Large Onion
  • Ground Beef (1lb)
  • Oregano (1 tbsp)
  • Crushed Tomatoes (28oz)
  • Garlic (3 cloves)
  • Tomato Paste (2 tbsp)
  • Fresh Parsley
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Spaghetti (1/2lb)
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat
  2. Add onion and saute until golden. Add garlic and fry
  3. Add ground beef, fry for ~8 minutes. Add oregano and tomato paste
  4. Add tomatoes, parsley and bring to a boil. Season with salt and pepper
  5. Reduce heat to low, cook for 15 minutes
  6. Cook spaghetti, drain and serve with sauce and cheese
